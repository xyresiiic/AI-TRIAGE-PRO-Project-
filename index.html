About AI Triage Pro

🚑

AI Triage Pro v2.0

A multimodal emergency dispatch system combining state-of-the-art speech recognition and computer vision to assist emergency responders in real-time triage decisions.

🎙️

OpenAI Whisper

Tiny model for efficient speech-to-text. Detects medical keywords from emergency calls with acoustic fingerprinting to identify synthetic voices.

openai-whisper
👁️

OpenAI CLIP

Zero-shot image classification using ViT-B/32. Classifies injury severity without task-specific training — healthy skin to severe trauma.

clip-vit-base-patch32
⚖️

Fusion Engine

Trust-aware weighted fusion combines audio and vision. Suspicious signals get reduced weight. Both-suspicious penalty shifts score up.

custom weighted fusion
🐍

Flask Backend

Lightweight Python REST API with three endpoints. Windows-safe temp file handling with lazy model loading and full traceback logging.

flask + flask-cors
SCORING SYSTEM
Score 1 — CRITICAL

Immediate life threat. Dispatch within 2 minutes.

Score 2 — URGENT

Serious condition. Priority dispatch within 10 minutes.

Score 3 — MODERATE

Needs medical attention. Non-emergency transport.

Score 4 — MINOR

Minor injury. Walk-in clinic appropriate.

Score 5 — STABLE

No emergency. Routine GP visit recommended.

DISCLAIMER

AI Triage Pro is a decision-support tool only. All dispatch decisions must be validated by qualified emergency medical personnel. Do not rely solely on automated scoring for life-critical decisions.
